Our Legacy & Ambience

Where Gastronomy Meets the Skyline

Perched high above Jaipur's glittering silhouette, Sky Grill is more than a restaurant — it is an elevated ritual. Born out of a passion for luxury wood-fired cooking and refined romance, we unite celestial heights with artisanal culinary excellence.

Our master chefs select exclusively organic, premium cuts of meats, sustainable seafood, and rare herbs to create plates that delight the eye and awaken the senses. Complemented by curated jazz music and fireside tables, every evening is a tailored symphony.
